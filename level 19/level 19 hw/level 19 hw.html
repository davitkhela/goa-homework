Real Steel

About the Movie

"Real Steel" is a 2011 American science fiction sports film starring Hugh Jackman. The movie is set in a future where robots box in the ring.

Cast

  • Hugh Jackman as Charlie Kenton
  • Dakota Goyo as Max Kenton
  • Evangeline Lilly as Bailey Tallet

Interesting Facts

The movie "Real Steel" is based on Richard Matheson's short story "Steel".
