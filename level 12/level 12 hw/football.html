Football

In Football There are many versions of that game.

American football

In American football, teams focus on strategic plays and physical confrontations, aiming to advance the ball to the end zone.

Rugby football

Rugby is a close-contact team sport with two main versions:

  1. Rugby Union
    • Played with 15 players per team, aiming to score tries (worth 5 points) by grounding the ball behind the opponent’s try line. Passing is backward or sideways, and kicking is used to move the ball forward.
  2. Rugby League
    • A separate code with distinct rules, including play-the-ball. It’s played professionally and pays players.
  3. Rugby Sevens
    • the exciting Olympic version played with 7 players per side!
